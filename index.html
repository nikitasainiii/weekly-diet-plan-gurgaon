Essential Supplements

Vitamin D3 + K2
600–1,000 IU daily (RDA) · 2,000 IU if deficient · Take with breakfast (fat meal) · Critical: deficiency extremely common in India
Vitamin B12
2.4 µg daily (RDA) · 500 µg common for maintenance · Morning with water · Essential for vegetarians
Iron (elemental)
18 mg daily (RDA) · Take with Vit C (amla/lemon) on empty stomach · Do not take with milk or calcium
Omega-3 (DHA/EPA)
250–500 mg daily · Take with dinner · Supports heart, brain, and joint health · Use algae-based for vegetarian
Zinc
8 mg daily (RDA) · With food · Supports immunity + wound healing · Take separate from iron/calcium
Magnesium Glycinate
310–320 mg daily (RDA) · Take at bedtime · Supports muscle recovery and sleep quality
Whey / Soy Protein
20–25 g post-workout · Use only if protein target (90g) is not met via whole foods
Multivitamin (Women's)
1 tablet with breakfast · Insurance against gaps · Ensure it covers 100% of most B-vitamins
Supplement timing note: Take iron at least 2 hours away from calcium supplements or dairy. B12 and D3 can be taken together at breakfast. Magnesium at night. Zinc with lunch or dinner.
During fasting (Thu 6 PM–Sat 12 AM): skip iron and zinc supplements. Continue D3, B12, and magnesium glycinate only if tolerated without food.

Immunity & Wellness

Daily immunity stack: Turmeric milk at night · Amla (fresh or murabba — non-sour form) daily · 5 soaked almonds every morning · Tulsi leaves in warm water 2–3×/week
Hydration: 2.5–3 L water daily · Electrolytes after gym (nimbu pani with rock salt, no soda) · ORS once a week if sweating heavily

Practical Reminders

  • Pre-cook: Soak rajma/chana overnight; batch-cook dal 2× per week to save time.
  • Post-workout window: Eat within 45–60 min after gym for muscle repair.
  • Sleep: 10–11 hrs is excellent. Ensure last meal is ≥1 hr before sleep.
  • Weigh-in: Check weight weekly (same time, morning, after toilet) to track progress. Target +0.25–0.5 kg/month.
